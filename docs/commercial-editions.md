# AlmaBridge Editions

AlmaBridge uses an open-core business model. Community Edition is open-source and self-hostable. Commercial editions provide the additional capabilities required for scale, assurance, integration, governance and support-backed deployment.

## Community Edition

Free and open-source.

Best for:

- Pilots
- Proofs of concept
- Self-hosted experimentation
- Small credential projects
- Developer testing

Includes:

- Basic credential issuing
- Learner dashboard
- Issuer dashboard
- Public verification
- Basic revocation
- QR verification
- Blockchain reference recording
- Basic audit log

## AlmaBridge Pro

For institutions issuing credentials at scale.

Includes:

- White-labelling
- Bulk issuing
- Moodle integration
- Advanced templates
- Issuer credit management
- Email workflows
- Enhanced reporting
- Support-backed onboarding

## AlmaBridge Assurance

For regulated, quality-assured and multi-institution environments.

Includes:

- Issuer Trust Registry
- Accreditation metadata
- Jurisdiction tagging
- Regulator dashboard
- Lifecycle governance
- ESCO skills and occupation mapping
- Verification analytics
- Assurance evidence packs
- Advanced audit exports

## AlmaBridge Enterprise

For government, sector-wide and enterprise infrastructure.

Includes:

- Multi-tenant deployment
- SSO
- Dedicated hosting
- Advanced APIs
- Sovereign deployment options
- SLA
- Security assurance
- Custom integrations

## Conversion Path

The intended adoption path is:

1. Developer or institution discovers AlmaBridge Community Edition.
2. They install and test credential issuing and verification.
3. They run a pilot or internal proof of concept.
4. They identify the need for branding, bulk issuing, Moodle integration, reporting, assurance or support.
5. They upgrade to AlmaBridge Pro, Assurance or Enterprise.
